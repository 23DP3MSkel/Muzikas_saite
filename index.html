Music facts

Music Triggers the Same Brain Pleasure as Food etc.

Listening to music releases dopamine, the "feel-good" chemical in the brain, in the same way that eating delicious food or experiencing pleasure does. This is why music can give you chills, euphoria, or deep emotional reactions.

Music Can Improve Physical Performance

Studies show that listening to upbeat music during exercise can:
  • Increase endurance by up to 15%
  • Reduce perceived effort (makes workouts feel easier)
  • Enhance motivation and performance
Music isn’t just entertainment—it’s deeply wired into our biology and culture! 🎶✨

The World’s Oldest Known Musical Instrument is 40,000 Years Old

Archaeologists discovered a flute made from a vulture’s bone in a German cave, dating back to the Paleolithic era. This suggests that music has been an essential part of human culture since prehistoric times.
